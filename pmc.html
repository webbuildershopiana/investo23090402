Portfolio Management System
Portfolio management refers to the professional management of a collection of investments, known as a portfolio, to achieve specific financial goals and objectives. It involves the selection, monitoring, and adjustment of various assets, such as stocks, bonds, and mutual funds, based on the investor's risk tolerance, time horizon, and financial targets. The aim is to optimize returns while minimizing risk to create a well-balanced and diversified investment strategy.
