Health Insurance
Health insurance is a type of insurance policy that provides financial coverage for medical expenses and healthcare services. Policyholders pay regular premiums to the insurer, and in return, the insurer covers a portion or the entire cost of medical treatments, hospitalization, prescription drugs, and other healthcare services, helping individuals and families manage healthcare costs and access necessary medical care.
