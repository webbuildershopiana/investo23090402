Term Insurance
Term insurance is a type of life insurance policy that provides coverage for a specific term or period, typically ranging from 5 to 30 years. If the insured person passes away during the term, the beneficiaries receive a death benefit payout. Unlike permanent life insurance, term insurance does not accumulate cash value and is usually more affordable, making it a popular choice for providing financial protection to loved ones during critical periods.
